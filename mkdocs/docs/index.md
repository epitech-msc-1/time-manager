# Welcome to Time Manager

![PrimeBank Logo](../assets/images/prime-bank.png){ align=right width=200 .theme-logo }

**Time Manager** is a comprehensive internal tool designed for **PrimeBank** to centralize time tracking, team management, and performance analysis. It streamlines the coordination between technical teams and support services, ensuring accurate time reporting and efficient project tracking.

---

## :material-rocket-launch: Key Features

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-clock-time-four-outline: **Time Tracking**
    ---
    Automated reporting of arrival and departure times for all employees with historical tracking.

-   :material-account-group: **Team Management**
    ---
    Manage teams, assign roles, and view collective schedules and dashboards.

-   :material-chart-box: **Performance KPIs**
    ---
    Visualize productivity metrics, overtime, and punctuality with interactive charts.

-   :material-shield-check: **Secure Access**
    ---
    Role-based access control (Manager vs. Employee) with JWT authentication.

</div>
